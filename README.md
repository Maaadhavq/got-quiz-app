# 🐉 Game of Thrones Quiz App 🐺

*"When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die... well, in this case, you just get a score at the end."* 👑

Welcome to the ultimate test of your Westeros knowledge! This is a fun, lightweight quiz app built for the club to see who truly deserves to sit on the Iron Throne (and who belongs in the Night's Watch). 

## ⚔️ What is this?
It's a web-based quiz game that challenges your memory of alliances, betrayals, dragons, and everything in between from the epic world of *Game of Thrones*. 

## 🍷 Features
- **Brain-Bending Questions:** Ranging from "I barely watched season 1" to "I am the Three-Eyed Raven".
- **Instant Verdicts:** Find out immediately if your knowledge is sharper than Valyrian steel.
- **Sleek UI:** We didn't have time to hire the maesters of the Citadel, so we built a clean and intuitive interface ourselves. 

## 🛡️ How to Play
Just open the website, channel your inner Tyrion Lannister ("I drink and I know things"), and answer some questions!
