01
Log‑transform is essential
Dengue cases are heavy-tailed (max=724). Log1p prevents the loss from chasing rare extreme values.
02
Skip connections beat bigger models
With 132 training samples, raw lags and rolling means via skip outperform deeper architectures.
03
Feature engineering > architecture
Rolling means improved R² more than doubling hidden dims or adding LSTM layers.
04
Compress BioBERT aggressively
768‑dim embeddings drown out 13 tabular features. Projecting to 16 dims restores balance.
